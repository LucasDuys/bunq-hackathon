# 04 -- CSRD ESRS E1 and the E1-7 Disclosure

_Status: stub. This is the compliance story the pitch hinges on._

## Questions

- Which waves apply? (Wave 1 = large public-interest, Wave 2 = large non-public-interest, Wave 3 = large-listed-SME.)
- What does E1-7 ("GHG removals and GHG mitigation projects financed through carbon credits") actually require?
- Can a *reserved-but-not-yet-retired* credit be disclosed? If not, how do we frame the reserve?
- What is the minimum field set for an offset record to be auditor-acceptable?
- Who audits CSRD? (Statutory auditors + limited-assurance regime.)
- What happens if the emission estimate is uncertain -- is a range acceptable?

## Key fields likely required in E1-7

- Project name, type (removal vs reduction), verification standard, vintage, registry id.
- Tonnes of CO2e claimed.
- Location (EU vs non-EU).
- Retirement status (retired vs held).
- Link to the retirement certificate.
- Price paid.
- Whether claimed inside scope (insetting) or outside (offsetting).

The pitch claim is not "we file your CSRD report for you." It is: "we produce the structured, auditable evidence that plugs directly into the E1-7 section of your CSRD report." This is narrower, defensible, and fits the hackathon timeframe.
