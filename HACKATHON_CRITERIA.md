# bunq HTFDI Hackathon — Scoring Criteria

> Source: judging-rubric slide (HTFDI Score Points), captured 2026-04-25.

| # | Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------|
| 1 | **Creativity & Innovation** | 25% | Originality of the idea. Novel application of AI agents or bunq API. |
| 2 | **Impact & Usefulness** | 30% | Does it solve a real problem or create significant value/fun? Potential impact. |
| 3 | **Technical Execution** | 20% | How well is the agent/application built? Functionality, robustness. Clever use of tools/APIs. |
| 4 | **bunq Integration** | 15% | How well does the feature integrate with bunq application and features? |
| 5 | **Presentation / Pitch** | 10% | Clarity of the presentation. Effectiveness of the demo. How well is the concept communicated? |

**Total weight:** 100%
